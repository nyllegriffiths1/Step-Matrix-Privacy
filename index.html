üì± How Your Data Works

Most data processing happens locally on your device:

  • Your iPhone's Health app collects step data from motion sensors
  • Step Matrix reads this data with your explicit permission
  • XP, levels, and achievement progress calculated locally on your device
  • Anonymous step counts sent to secure database for leaderboard rankings only
  • System generates anonymous ID (like "USER_ABC123") for leaderboard display
  • All personal app settings and progress save to your device storage only
  • Delete the app = all local Step Matrix data disappears, leaderboard entry becomes inactive
